Information Collection & Use

At Nirman Developers, we are committed to respecting and protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy details the types of personal information we collect through our website and how we use, process, and protect it.

1. Personal Details You Provide

We collect information that you send to us voluntarily when filling out our enquiry forms, subscribing to our newsletter, or requesting property brochures. This may include your name, phone number, email address, current city, and specific property requirements.

2. How We Process the Information

We use your contact details to provide the property brochures, project updates, pricing list sheets, or municipal compliance documents you requested. Your details are never sold to external third-party advertisers.

3. Security of Data

We implement standard physical and electronic security procedures to safeguard your details from unauthorized access, modification, or exposure.
